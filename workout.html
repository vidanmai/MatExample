Workout Philosophy

The Beginning

We all have to start from somewhere. My fitness journey started as a result of continous comments on my body which ultimately shaped a negative body image in my mind. Although modern society cherishes values and traits in people that are accepting of one's own body and other bodies, this couldn't be further from the truth. In this case, the truth of 'Everyone is different' became very true to me. I used to be very skinny and had a very small frame. I was the smallest guy. Many comments were encouraging to gain weight, but other comments were said with ill intent and was a way for people to gain superiority.
